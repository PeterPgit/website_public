hello! if you're reading this, it means you've found my website one way or another. thanks for checking things out! to be honest, I don't quite have an exact reason for making this website in the first place; it just seemed like it would be fun and gets me to learn something new. as noted in the home page, this all started out as a quarantine project. it all started from a blank page and slowly but surely, with many, many tutorials + youtube videos later, developed into what you're seeing today.

I'll sporadically update this from time to time with whatever pops into my head. this'll act as a page for me to express all of my ideas and stuff that I constantly think about.

as for starters, you've probably noticed the grammar in which I type everything out. that's not because I'm lazy or anything, but instead because I feel like it adds voice to my writing. because of the nature of communicating online, it can be hard to communicate and interpret emotion within a piece of text. the lack of capitalization serves to both denote informal writing and sets the tone of the words.


for example, a sentence like:

Hello, how are you doing today?

feels formal, less unique, and is the standard writing.

however,

hello, how are you doing today?

provokes a different emotion. the lack of fomality instead feels like a friend talking to a friend. it's more soft spoken and feels more genuine.


some other examples include:

HELLO, HOW ARE YOU DOING TODAY?

to denote anger, or its real life equivalent of screaming

and

hElLo, HoW aRe YoU dOiNg ToDaY?

to denote sarcasm.


the goal of this website is to act as a place for me to talk about different projects, goals, and interests. I want it to lack formality and to instead act as a way for people to see some insight on my life. it's a place for me to express different feelings and explain my thoughts on different topics. it's as if I'm talking to a friend, yet that friend is a random person behind a screen.
