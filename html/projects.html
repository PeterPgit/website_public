  • MC StatTrak

    • MC StatTrak is my first project outside of this website. it mimics Counter Strike: Global Offensive's StatTrak system, which tracks the amount of kills you get on a weapon. I recreated this design (with the help / inspiration of another creator) and implemented tracking for an assortment of tools and weapons, along with toggleable player-specific customization, tracking everything from specific blocks mined to weapon kills. if you've ever played Minecraft, I encourage you to check out my datapack!
